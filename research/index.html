Sound Familiar?

Research Shouldn't Feel This Hard

Most researchers hit the same wall. You have the data, the question, and the deadline — but the analysis is the bottleneck.

"I don't know which statistical test to use for my data."

Choosing the wrong model can undermine your entire methodology. You need someone who understands the difference and when it matters for your specific study design.

"The deadline is real and the analysis still isn't done."

Whether it's a defense date, a manuscript submission, or a grant report — unfinished or unreliable analysis creates real risk. It needs to be clean, correct, and defensible before it goes anywhere.

"I have the output but I'm not sure it's telling the right story."

Translating statistical output into clear, defensible language — for a committee, journal reviewers, or leadership — is its own specialized skill. Getting the interpretation wrong is just as costly as getting the analysis wrong.
