Sound Familiar?

Research Shouldn't Feel This Hard

Most graduate students and organizations hit the same wall. You have the data, but not the time, tools, or expertise to turn it into defensible results.

"I don't know which statistical test to use for my data."

Choosing the wrong model can undermine your entire methodology. You need someone who understands the difference and when it matters for your specific study design.

"My data is messy and my defense date is getting close."

Inconsistent variables, missing values, and disorganized responses are common, but they must be cleaned correctly before any analysis can be trusted by a committee.

"I have the output but I don't know how to write up the results."

Translating SPSS or R output into clear, committee-ready language, with defensible interpretation and proper formatting, is its own specialized skill set.
