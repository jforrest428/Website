The Problem

The Information Exists. It's Just Scattered Across Five Apps You Don't Have Time to Check.

A service business generates more operational data than any one person can reliably track: call volume, booking rates, job completions, unanswered leads, review activity, outstanding follow-ups, upcoming capacity, and cash flow signals. The problem isn't a lack of data. It's that nobody's pulling it together for you.

The result is that most owner-operators run on intuition and lag. They find out call volume dropped when they notice the schedule looks thin on Thursday. They find out a review problem exists when a family member happens to search the business name on Google. They find out revenue is soft when they look at the bank balance at the end of the month, not in time to do anything about it.

The information to catch all of this early is already there. It just lives in your call log, your scheduling app, your Yelp dashboard, and your CRM, and nobody has the time to check all four before the day starts.

The Owner's Daily Briefing pulls it together into a single plain-English summary, delivered to your phone by 7 AM. Read it in 60 seconds. Know where your business stands. Move on.

How It Works

Collect. Summarize. Deliver.

01

Collect

Each morning, we pull data from your connected sources, call logs, booking system, review platforms, and any additional integrations. No manual entry, no CSV exports required.

02

Summarize

We distill everything into plain English: what happened yesterday, what needs attention today, anything that looks unusual. Numbers are contextualized, not just listed, so you know what actually matters.

03

Deliver

It lands in your inbox or as a text by 7 AM, every business day. Read it at the job site, on your phone before the crew arrives, or over coffee. No logins. No dashboards. Just the information you need.
