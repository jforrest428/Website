Common Questions

Before You Fill Out the Form

What do I actually need to set this up?

Almost nothing on your end. You need an active phone number (your existing business line is fine) and access to your Google Business Profile for the review tools. No new software to learn, no dashboard to log into every day. I handle the full setup and configure everything to your business — your services, pricing, hours, and tone.

How long until it's live?

Most setups go live within 5–7 business days of the audit call. The free audit is where we figure out what you actually need — after that, I build and configure everything while you run your business. There's no long implementation process. You'll get a confirmation when it's running and a test message so you can see it work firsthand.

Is there a contract? What if I want to cancel?

No long-term contract. Monthly plans run month-to-month and you can cancel with 30 days' notice. The setup fee covers my time to configure and integrate everything — that's a one-time cost regardless of how long you stay. If a tool isn't performing after 60 days, reach out and we'll figure out why together before you decide to cancel.

Do you work with businesses outside of plumbing?

Yes — the tools were designed around plumbing data because that's a high-call-volume, high-urgency business that stress-tests every edge case. But the same problems (missed calls, ignored estimates, lapsed customers, unanswered reviews) show up in every service trade. HVAC, electrical, roofing, landscaping, cleaning, auto repair — if your phone is your pipeline, these tools apply to you.

How do I know it'll actually work for my business?

That's exactly what the free audit is for. I don't pitch products blind — I look at your actual call volume, your review situation, your estimate close rate, and your customer list before recommending anything. If the data doesn't support a tool paying for itself in your specific situation, I'll tell you that before you spend a dollar. The audit is genuinely free and there's no commitment required.

What's the difference between this and hiring a marketing agency?

Marketing agencies generate new leads. These tools recover revenue you're already earning — from calls you're already getting but missing, estimates you're already sending but not following up on, and customers who already trust you but haven't heard from you in 18 months. Most service businesses have $20,000–$40,000 in recoverable revenue sitting in their existing data before they ever need to spend on ads.
