The Problem

Half Your Website Visitors Leave Without Saying a Word.

Websites with real-time chat capture 3–5x more leads than those without. Most service business owners have no idea this gap exists — because visitors who leave without contacting you don't show up anywhere. No missed call log. No voicemail. Just a visitor who landed, didn't find what they needed, and moved on.

Your phone works when someone dials it. But a growing share of customers — especially those booking evenings, weekends, or over lunch — won't call. They'll load your site, scan your services, have a question they can't quickly answer, and bail. No form submission. No callback request. They're gone, and you'll never know they were there.

This problem is invisible, which makes it expensive. You could be paying for SEO, running Google ads, getting referrals — and leaking a third of that traffic the moment a visitor has a question your static website can't answer. Your competitors who have a chat widget are quietly capturing the leads your site is letting walk.

The AI Website Chatbot closes that gap. It sits on your site 24/7, answers questions immediately using your actual service list, your pricing, your hours, your service area — and when a visitor is ready to book, it captures their info and gets them on your calendar. No staff required. No after-hours answering service. No calls you have to return the next morning.

Common Questions

A Few Things People Usually Ask

What if my customers don't like talking to a chatbot?

Most don't know they are. The chatbot is configured to respond in your business's voice — no "I am an AI assistant" preamble, no robotic phrasing. It reads like someone who works for you and knows the business cold. For customers who insist on talking to a person, the chatbot is set up to take their number and let them know you'll call back. Nobody gets a dead end.

Do I need to do anything technical?

No. We handle the full setup — building and configuring the chatbot, training it on your business info, and installing the widget on your site. If your site is on Squarespace, WordPress, or Wix, it's a single line of code we walk you through pasting in. You don't touch the configuration unless you want to. When your hours change or you add a new service, you email us and we update it.

What happens when the chatbot can't answer something?

It's honest about it. If a visitor asks something outside of what the chatbot knows, it says so and offers to take their contact details so you can follow up directly. It never makes up an answer, never gives a wrong price, and never promises something you haven't authorized it to say. We build those guardrails in during setup.

Can I see what conversations are happening?

Yes. Every conversation is logged and available to you. Starter gets a monthly summary. Pro gets weekly reports breaking down conversation volume, leads captured, and the most common questions visitors are asking — which is often the most valuable part, since it tells you what's missing from your website. Premium gets real-time notifications so you know the moment a qualified lead comes in.
