About

Forrest Analytics Group

Forrest Analytics Group is a data analytics and research consulting firm focused on transparent analysis for academic and applied decision-making.

We provide end-to-end analytical support, including survey design, data preparation, statistical modeling, hypothesis testing, and interpretation of results.

Recent work includes designing and analyzing a large-scale survey study for a doctoral dissertation, supporting instrument validation, statistical modeling, and interpretation.

We work with doctoral researchers, academic departments, and small organizations that require reliable analytical expertise without the overhead of a full-time analyst — specializing in projects where data quality, statistical validity, and clear communication are critical.
