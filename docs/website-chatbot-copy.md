**Q: What if my customers don't like talking to a chatbot?**
```
Most don't know they are. The chatbot is configured to respond in your business's voice — no "I am an AI assistant" preamble, no robotic phrasing. It reads like someone who works for you and knows the business cold. For customers who insist on talking to a person, the chatbot is configured to capture their number and let them know you'll call back. Nobody gets a dead end.
```

---

**Q: Do I need to do anything technical?**
```
No. We handle the full setup — building the chatbot, training it on your business info, and installing the widget on your site. If your site is on Squarespace, WordPress, or Wix, it's a single line of code that we walk you through pasting in. You don't touch the configuration unless you want to. When your hours change or you add a new service, you email us and we update it.
```

---

**Q: What happens when the chatbot can't answer something?**
```
It's honest about it. If a visitor asks something outside of what the chatbot knows, it tells them it doesn't have that information and offers to take their contact details so you can follow up directly. It never makes up an answer, never gives a wrong price, and never promises something you haven't authorized it to say. We build the boundaries in during setup.
```

---

**Q: Can I see what conversations are happening?**
```
Yes. Every conversation is logged and available to you. On the Starter plan, you get a monthly summary. On Pro, weekly reports that break down conversation volume, leads captured, and the most common questions visitors are asking — which is often the most useful part, because it tells you what's missing from your website. On Premium, you can get real-time notifications so you know the moment a qualified lead comes in.
```
